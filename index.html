Bio

Yuyang Ma is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Lehigh University. He received a master's degree in operations research from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2022. Prior to that, he received a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering from the University of Pittsburgh (Pittsburgh, United States) and a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering from Sichuan University (Chengdu, China). Yuyang's research interests lie in data-driven optimization and optimization problems in machine learning. He is currently working with Dr. Karmel S. Shehadeh.
